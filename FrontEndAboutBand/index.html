THE BAND

We love music

We are a Korean girl group founded and managed by YG Entertainment. The group consists of 4 members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé and Lisa. Blackpink officially debuted with the first single album titled "Square One", including 2 songs "Boombayah" and "Whistle". The meaning of the name Blackpink is against the popular perception of pink. Pink is often used to express beauty, but Blackpink means "Beauty is not everything". It also symbolizes that they are a group that not only possesses beautiful looks but is also extremely talented. capacity and personality.
